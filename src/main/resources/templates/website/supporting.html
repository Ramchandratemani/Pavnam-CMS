Supporting the Cause with PETA

PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals) is one of the world’s largest animal rights organizations, committed to protecting animals from cruelty and exploitation. Since 2000, PETA India has championed compassion and awareness in the country.

Key Focus Areas:

  • 🌱 Promoting Veganism for Ethical and Environmental Benefits
  • 🎭 Advocating Against Animal Use in Entertainment
  • 🛑 Campaigning to End Animal Cruelty in Fashion and Testing
  • 📜 Strengthening Animal Welfare Legislation

Explore more about their inspiring campaigns like "Adopt, Don’t Shop," "Meat-Free India," and their fight for cruelty-free fashion and ethical treatment of animals.

Learn More at PETA India
