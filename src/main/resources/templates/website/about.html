About Us

Our Driving Force

Mission

To uphold and promote the principles of purity, hygiene, and vegetarianism in the food industry by providing businesses with a trusted certification that ensures they meet the highest standards of contamination-free and ethical practices.

Vision

To become a global benchmark for vegetarian food quality, fostering trust and confidence among consumers while encouraging businesses to adopt sustainable, ethical, and hygienic practices that prioritize health and the environment.

Values

At Pavnam, we are driven by a commitment to integrity, excellence, sustainability, and respect for life. Our core values reflect our dedication to fostering a vegetarian lifestyle while encouraging ethical business practices and consumer trust

Idea Behind Pavnam

The idea behind Pavnam is rooted in creating a dependable assurance system for vegetarian consumers. For individuals who strictly adhere to vegetarianism, finding restaurants or food outlets that genuinely avoid serving non-vegetarian food can be challenging. This lack of transparency often leads to uncertainty and compromises trust in dining choices.


Pavnam Certification was conceptualized to address this need. It serves as a bridge between vegetarian consumers and food establishments by certifying restaurants and outlets that meet strict criteria for serving 100% vegetarian food. The certification process involves rigorous checks to ensure compliance with vegetarian standards, eliminating the risk of cross-contamination or accidental inclusion of non-vegetarian ingredients.


Ultimately, Pavnam isn't just a certification; it’s a movement to simplify dining choices for vegetarians and ensure that their values are respected without compromise.
