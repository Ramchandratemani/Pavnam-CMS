Frequently Asked Question?

Pavnam provides a trusted certification process backed by rigorous standards, expert evaluations, and a commitment to fostering trust in the food and nutrition industry. Our team ensures a seamless, professional, and efficient certification journey.

The purpose of Pavnam certification is to validate and promote pure vegetarian, high-quality, safe, and ethical food products, empowering consumers to make informed choices while supporting producers in meeting global food standards.

- Builds consumer trust and confidence in your products for pure vegetarian food.
- Enhances marketability and brand recognition.
- Helps meet regulatory and international compliance requirements.
- Positions your brand as a leader in quality and safety.

Our certification process includes detailed assessments, on-site audits, and laboratory testing (where applicable) to ensure that products meet established quality, safety, and ethical standards.

Certification fees depend on the scope, category, and volume of products being certified. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

- Product / Shop specifications and descriptions.
- Manufacturing process details.
- Quality control reports.
- Applicable regulatory compliance certificates.
- Business registration and tax identification.

The certification timeline depends on the complexity of the product and adherence to required standards. Generally, it takes between 2-6 weeks after submission of all necessary documents and completion of the audit.

- Vegan Food: Excludes all animal products, including dairy, eggs, and honey.
- Vegetarian Food: Excludes meat and fish but may include dairy, eggs, and honey.

- Submit an application and provide required documentation.
- Undergo initial review and assessment by our team.
- Schedule an on-site audit or product testing (if necessary).
- Address any non-compliance issues.
- Receive your certification upon successful completion of the process.

Any business, organization, or individual involved in the production, manufacturing, or distribution of food products that meets the required standards can apply for certification. Eligibility is determined during the initial application review.

Pavnam certifies a wide range of food products, including but not limited to:
- Shops and Cart Vendors
- Resturants and Hotels
- Packaged and processed foods.
- Fresh produce.
- Vegan and vegetarian products.
- Organic and health foods.
- Beverages and dietary supplements.

If a product does not meet certification requirements, we provide a detailed report highlighting non-compliance areas. The applicant can address the issues and reapply for certification.

Certification validity typically ranges from 1 to 3 years, depending on the type of certification. Details about the validity period will be provided upon issuance of the certification.

Yes, certifications can be renewed. The renewal process involves a reassessment of your product or facility, including document reviews and audits. It’s recommended to initiate the renewal process at least 3 months before the expiration date.
